Research

Secure, intelligent, and human-centered systems

My research program integrates trustworthy AI, cybersecurity, neurotechnology, biometrics, and networked systems.

AI

Trustworthy and Responsible AI

Methods for secure, explainable, robust, fair, privacy-aware, and accountable AI. Current interests include neuro-symbolic reasoning, agentic AI governance, uncertainty-aware learning, and evaluation of high-impact AI systems.

Neuro-AI and Brain Computing

EEG-based biometric authentication, stress inference, multimodal brain decoding, signal-quality-aware learning, personalization, and assistive communication.

SEC

Cybersecurity and Biometrics

AI-driven intrusion detection, authentication, privacy-preserving learning, adversarial resilience, explainable cyber defense, and human-centered security.

IoT

IoT, IoV, UAVs, and Cyber-Physical Systems

Secure routing, resilient communications, intelligent transportation, healthcare IoT, autonomous networks, edge intelligence, and next-generation industrial systems.
