Teaching philosophy

Three principles

Connect theory to evidence

Students should see how protocols, models, and security assumptions behave in real data and systems.

Build responsible judgment

Technical competence includes understanding uncertainty, privacy, fairness, risks, and consequences.

Create research pathways

Assignments and modules should prepare students to ask questions, reproduce results, and participate in research.
